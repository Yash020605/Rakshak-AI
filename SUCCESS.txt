🆘 TROUBLESHOOTING:

If app crashes:
- Grant all permissions (Location, Camera, Storage)
- Check Settings → Apps → Rakshak AI → Permissions

If alerts don't appear on dashboard:
- Refresh the dashboard page
- Check backend is running: http://localhost:5000/health

If SMS doesn't send:
- Check Twilio credentials in backend/.env
- Verify phone numbers are in E.164 format (+919876543210)
